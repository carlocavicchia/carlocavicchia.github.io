ongoing projects

my current researches are:

  • Cavicchia, C., Vichi, M. and Zaccaria, G.
    Gaussian mixture model with an extended ultrametric covariance structure
    the maximum likelihood estimation of a Gaussian Mixture Model where the covariance structure is modeled according to an extended ultrametric covariance matrix, the model extends the concepts and definitions of ultrametric matrix in order to model generic covariance structure. (Submitted)

  • Cavicchia, C., Vichi, M. and Zaccaria, G.
    Hierarchical Disjoint Principal Component Analysis
    a new methodology which extends the Disjoint Principal Component Analysis (DPCA) to find out about different grades of syntheses, from the lowest level of the hierarchy upwards, it is formulated in a semi-parametric least-squares framework. (Submitted)

  • Triassi, M., De Rosa, E., Giovinetti, M.C., Montuori, P., Sarnacchiaro, P. and Cavicchia, C.
    Environmental quality assessment and estimates of heavy metal loads into the central Mediterranean sea from Volturno river, the river of the “land of fires” area, souther Italy
    in order to evaluate the heavy metals pollution in the Volturno river and its environmental impact on the Tyrrhenian Sea, eight heavy metals were determined in the water dissolved phase, suspended particulate matter and sediment samples collected from 10 sites in different seasons; in relation to the ecological assessment, the Volturno river and its estuary would be considered as an area in which the ecological integrity is possibly at risk. (Submitted)

  • Cavicchia, C., Vichi, M. and Zaccaria, G.
    Composite indicators for climate change domains and their nested relations in the European Union
    climate change is a compelling topic in public debate because of the risks in human life; a new analysis of the nested relationships among the aforementioned areas via a hierarchical model-based composite indicator construction is presented by analysing in-depth the causes and the efforts to combat climate change of the 28 European Countries.

  • Vichi, M., Groenen, P.J.F. and Cavicchia, C.
    Hierachical Means Clustering
    a novel method called Hierarchical Means Clustering (HMC) that extends K-means to the case of hierarchical clustering by producing a hierarchy that is formed by n nested partitions into one to n clusters with minimal total cluster deviance; in addition HMC can be estimated for a limited number of partitions by defining a parsimonious tree.

  • Cavicchia, C., Scrucca, L. and Vichi, M.
    Model-based Parsimonious Hierarchical Clustering
    the maximum likelihood estimation of Hierachical Means Clustering that extends the GMM (model EII) to the case of hierarchical clustering by producing a hierarchy that is formed by n nested partitions.

  • Cavicchia, C., Palini, F. and Vichi, M.
    MATLAB package for Clustering Disjoint Principal Component Analysis with Cross-loadings and Constraints on Units, Variables and Components
    a MATLAB package for the least-squares estimation of the model Clustering and Disjoint Principal Component Analysis is proposed with some additional options to conduct confirmatory analysis by constraining the association between some variables and some components, and/or pre-specifying the belonging of some units to some clusters options, and to increase the goodness of fit of the model by adding cross-loadings.
